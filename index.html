FAQ

1. How do the registration process of the panel discussion happens?

There is a Website related to the project INCODE-X. There you can find the registration link for the panel discussion and You can register to the panel discussion using that given link.

2. What are the languages that can be used in the competition phase?

The competitors can create the app on android and ios using Java, Kotlin and C++ . As well you can use any language to code the app according to your preferences .

3. How will the final app be selected?

The top 20 apps (10 globally and 10 locally) will be selected by a judge panel considering the best innovative idea and the way that the app has been build up.
